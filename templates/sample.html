How did rates affect your Student Loan ReFi?

In general, it is best to refinance your loans before rates increase. This allows you to borrow funs at the lowest cost to a lender, passing the savings onto you!

After plummeting since 1980, rates have recently skyrocketed! If you refi'd around 2008, you may have saved quite a bundle!
